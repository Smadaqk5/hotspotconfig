Frequently Asked Questions

What types of tickets can I create?

You can create time-based tickets (1 hour, 1 day, 1 week) and data-based tickets (1GB, 5GB, 10GB). You can also set custom pricing for each ticket type.

How do tickets automatically expire?

Time-based tickets expire after the specified duration. Data-based tickets expire when the data limit is reached. The system automatically disables expired tickets in your MikroTik router.

Can I print tickets for my customers?

Yes! You can export tickets as PDF files for printing. We support both regular and thermal printers. You can also share tickets digitally via email or SMS.

How do I track my revenue?

Our dashboard shows real-time sales data, revenue tracking, and analytics. You can see daily, weekly, and monthly reports, plus detailed breakdowns by ticket type.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a 7-day free trial for all new users. No credit card required. You can generate up to 100 tickets during the trial period.
