Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

What types of tickets can I create?

You can create both time-based tickets (1 hour, 1 day, etc.) and data-based tickets (1GB, 5GB, etc.) with custom pricing.

How do tickets automatically expire?

Tickets automatically expire after the specified time duration or when the data limit is reached, ensuring fair usage.

Can I print tickets for my customers?

Yes! You can print tickets in various formats including standard A4 and thermal printer formats.

Is there a mobile app?

Our web application is fully responsive and works perfectly on mobile devices. No separate app needed!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-PESA, Airtel Money, and other mobile money services through Pesapal integration.
